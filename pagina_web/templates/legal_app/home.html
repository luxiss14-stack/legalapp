Legal Case Management System

Welcome to the Legal Management System. This application allows clients to register, submit legal issues, and communicate directly with assigned lawyers through a chat interface.

Quick Actions

Register a Client

Create a new client profile with contact information.

Register a Lawyer

Add a lawyer to the system.

View Clients

Browse all registered clients and their legal issues.

View Lawyers

Browse all registered lawyers.

System Features

  • Register clients.
  • Register lawyers.
  • A client may create multiple legal issues.
  • Each issue stores its own discussion history.
  • Lawyers can communicate with clients through chat.
  • All conversations remain attached to their corresponding issue.
